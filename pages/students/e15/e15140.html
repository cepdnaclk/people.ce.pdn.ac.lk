Bio

I graduated as a Computer Engineer (BSc. Hons) with First Class in 2021. I worked in the Department of Computer Engineering as a Temporary Instructor for around a year after my graduation. During that time I was able to help on several department's student-driven projects to improve the web presence of the Department, including projects.ce.pdn.ac.lk, people.ce.pdn.ac.lk, api.ce.pdn.ac.lk. Also I got a chance to support to establish ESCAL MakerSpace Lab.

Later I joined Rhino Partners as a Software Engineer, and Now I'm working there.

I'm interested in IoT, Embedded Systems, Robotics, Full Stack Web Development and Computer-Aided Designing and Manufacturing.
