Isuru received his B.Sc. degree in Engineering from the University of Peradeniya, Sri Lanka, in 2011, and his Ph.D. degree in Computer Science and Engineering from the University of New South Wales, Sydney, Australia, in 2016. He is currently a Senior Lecturer in Computer Engineering at the University of Peradeinya. He has a keen interest in improving the quality of Engineering Education and research. Isuru is currently involved in curriculum development and devising learner-centered delivery methods.

Research Interests:
  • Computer architectural optimizations
  • Application specific (multi-)processor systems
  • Design automation of microprocessor systems
  • FPGA-based hardware acceleration
  • Robotics and multi-agent intelligent systems
  • Biomedical engineering
