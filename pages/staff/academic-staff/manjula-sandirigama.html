Dr. Manjula Sandirigama received a Ph.D. in Computer Science from the University of Ehime, Japan (2001), an M.Sc. in Computer Science from the University of Ehime, Japan (1998), and a B.Sc. in Electrical Engineering from the University of Peradeniya, Sri Lanka (1991). 

He was appointed a Senior Lecturer at the University of Peradeniya in the Department of Computer Engineering and later promoted to Associate Professor. He is also an Attorney-at-Law, Sri Lanka. His primary expertise is in Secure Communication Protocols. He also has expertise in Intellectual Property Rights and teaches IP for general engineering students. He has authored a book on IP for science and technology students.
