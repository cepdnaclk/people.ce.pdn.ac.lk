Biswajith Dissanayake is a Lecturer in the Department of Computer Engineering, Faculty of Engineering, University of Peradeniya. His research interests include generative modeling, probabilistic programming and deep learning, with applications in machine learning for scientific discovery. His recent work focuses on Bayesian inference for large-scale simulators and distributed training at supercomputing scale. His teaching interests include Programming Languages, Machine Learning, Data Science, Computer Architecture, Computer Networks, Software Engineering, software architecture, Database systems and Cybersecurity. He has prior industry experience as an IT Specialist at IBM World Trade Corporation and has held academic appointments at Uva Wellassa University , University of Peradeniya and the CECB.

Educational Qualifications:

  • BSc(Eng) Hons degree in Computer Engineering
  • MSc degree in Information Technology (UoM)

Interested in working on :
