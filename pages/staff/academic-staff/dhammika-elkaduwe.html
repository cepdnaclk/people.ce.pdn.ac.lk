Dhammika Elkaduwe received his B.Sc (Eng) in 2002 from the Faculty of Engineering, University of Peradeniya, specializing in Electrical and Electronic Engineering. Soon after completing the Degree, he joined the Department of Computer Engineering, University of Peradeniya, as a temporary lecturer. He received his PhD from the University of New South Wales, Sydney, Australia. During his PhD, he was working on the seL4 kernel, which is the world's first formally verified operating system kernel. Dhammika is now serving the Department in the capacity of a Senior Lecturer. His primary expertise includes operating systems, microkernel-based systems, system security, and virtualization.
