Mahanama received his PhD in Computer Science and Engineering from The University of New South Wales, Australia and BSc Eng. in Computer Engineering from University of Peradeniya. He has post-doctoral industry experience as an Embedded Software Engineer and a Product Owner in the professional AV networking industry. Mahanama is also a certified SAFe 4 Agile Product and Solution Manager. His research interests include Memory System Optimisation, Micro-architectural improvements in Processor Systems and Energy Efficient Computing.
