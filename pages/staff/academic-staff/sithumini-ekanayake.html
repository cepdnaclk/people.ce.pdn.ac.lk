Sithumini Ekanayake received her B.Sc.(Eng.) degree in Electrical and Electronic Engineering from University of Peradeniya, Sri Lanka, in 2012, and the Ph.D. degree from The University of New South Wales, Australia, in 2018. Prior to joining the Computer Engineering department of University of Peradeniya, she was with the HV Motors and Drives department, Toshiba International Corporation Pty Ltd, Australia. Her research interests include Electric Drives, Power Electronics, Electrical Machines and Motion Control Systems.
